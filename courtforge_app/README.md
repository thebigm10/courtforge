# CourtForge

A mobile-first fitness and nutrition prototype for teenage basketball players.

## Open it
1. Unzip the folder.
2. Open `index.html` in Chrome, Safari, Edge, or Firefox.
3. On iPhone, use Safari → Share → Add to Home Screen for an app-like shortcut.

## Included
- Functional onboarding with athlete, equipment, nutrition, and knee-safety inputs
- Today dashboard, readiness score, streaks, XP, badges, checklist, water and sleep tracking
- Six-day home/gym workout split plus recovery day
- Exercise logging fields, technique notes, RIR guidance, and pain warnings
- Knee-pain check-ins and adaptive recommendations
- Food logging, calorie/protein tracking, nutrition score, and fast-food guide
- Progress trends, checkpoints, achievements, and profile editing
- Rules-based AI coach for immediate safe guidance
- Local browser storage, so entries remain saved on the device

## Important
This prototype is educational. It does not diagnose injuries or replace a doctor, physical therapist, registered dietitian, parent/guardian, or qualified coach.
